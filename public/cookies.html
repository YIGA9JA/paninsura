Cookie Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our site, and improve your browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the site to function properly, such as enabling logins and form submissions.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site, which pages are most visited, and detect issues.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language or region, to personalize your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your browsing activity to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly.
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior to improve our services.
  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Deliver personalized advertisements where applicable.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services, such as Google Analytics, to understand user activity on our site. These services may set their own cookies to collect information about your activities across websites.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and features of this website.

  • Browser Settings: Adjust settings in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari to manage cookie preferences.
  • Opt-Out Tools: Use tools like NAI opt-out to manage ad-related cookies.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies.
